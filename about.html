Our Story

Making life easy through fresh, quality produce

OUR MISSION

We Believe in Fresh, Healthy Living

Mainno's Trading Projects (PTY) Ltd is dedicated to making life easy by providing prepared, chopped, and cut fruits and vegetables to the community at large. We believe that food, especially fruits and vegetables, is essential to healthy living, and we're committed to delivering the freshest produce to your door.

ESTABLISHED EXCELLENCE

A Registered & Compliant Company

Mainno's Trading Projects (PTY) Ltd is a registered company as per CIPC requirements, with our premises strategically situated in the City of Tshwane, Lotus Gardens, Pretoria, Gauteng.

We are compliant in every respect in terms of HACCP and other health requirements with full traceability. Our core business is the supply of fresh fruit and vegetables, fresh peeled, chopped and cut vegetables, and salads to supermarkets and directly to customers.

GROWTH & JOBS

Creating Jobs, Growing Communities

We recognized that small business is the key to growing our country's economy and creating jobs. Today, we employ a dedicated team of over 12 people, and our fresh and fresh-cut vegetables are found in more than 50 supermarkets across Pretoria, Gauteng.

We're proud to contribute to our community by providing employment opportunities and making healthy eating more accessible and convenient for working professionals and families.

QUALITY ASSURANCE

HACCP Compliant with Full Traceability

Food safety and quality are at the heart of everything we do. Our facility is fully compliant with HACCP (Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points) standards, ensuring that every product meets the highest safety and quality benchmarks.

We maintain complete traceability from source to delivery, giving you peace of mind that your vegetables are safe, fresh, and of premium quality. Our commitment to health requirements and regulations means you can trust Mainno's for your family's meals.
