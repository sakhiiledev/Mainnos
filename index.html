Welcome to Mainno’s

Freshness. Convenience. Community.

Mainno’s Trading Projects (PTY) Ltd supplies fresh fruits and vegetables, including professionally prepared, peeled, and chopped produce for everyday convenience. Based in Lotus Gardens, Pretoria, we are committed to quality, food safety compliance, and supporting our community through job creation and reliable service.

OUR STORY

About Mainno's

Mainno's Trading Projects (PTY) Ltd is making life easy by providing prepared, chopped and cut fruits and vegetables to the community at large, while creating jobs and growth for individuals.

We started our business understanding that food, especially fruits and vegetables, is essential to healthy living. Today, we employ a dedicated team and our fresh and fresh-cut vegetables are found in most supermarkets across Pretoria, Gauteng.
