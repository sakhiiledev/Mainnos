About Mainnos

Mainno's making life easy by providing prepared, chopped and cut Fruits and Vegetables to community at large and also creating jobs and growth to individuals.

Mainno's Trading Projects (PTY) Ltd is a registered company as per CIPC requirements with its premises strategically situated in the City of Tshwane Lotus Garden in Pretoria Gauteng. We are compliant in every respect in terms of HACCP and other health requirements with full traceability. Our core business is the supply of fresh fruit and vegetables, fresh peeled, chopped and cut vegetables and salads.

We started our business understanding that food, especially fruits and vegetables, is essential to healthy living. We recognized that small business is the key to growing our country's economy and creating jobs. Today, we employ a dedicated team and our fresh and fresh-cut vegetables are found in most supermarkets across Pretoria, Gauteng.

Sustainability: The demand for fresh cut vegetables continues to grow due to convenience and health reasons. Strengths: We are a committed team dedicated to delivering quality products and services at all times. Our business is sustainable because working professionals need convenient meal solutions, and ready-cut vegetables provide their easiest way to healthy eating.
